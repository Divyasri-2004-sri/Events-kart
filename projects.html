Project Suchana

A competition for Planet Saviours aged 8–25 years

A competition-based project designed for Planet Saviours aged 8–25 years. "Suchana" means guidance in Sanskrit. Through project-based learning, young minds research global warming issues and implement real solutions aligned with UN Agenda 2050. The project follows the UN slogan — Leave No One Behind.

for a Sustainable Ocean

Project Suchana 2.0

A global Ocean Conference continuing the Suchana mission

A global Ocean Conference continuing the Suchana mission, bringing together students, researchers, and NGOs to protect marine ecosystems. Focused on UN SDGs 12, 13 and 14 — addressing ocean pollution, overfishing, habitat loss, and climate impact on marine life. Supported by ICO, Dept of Education, Philippines.
