Our Philosophy

Operating at the Intersection

EventsKart operates at the intersection of environmental conservation, professional training, and global tourism — hosting educational campaigns and workshops designed to bridge knowledge gaps and inspire action towards a sustainable future.

Meet Our Founder
Who We Are

Awareness is the foundation of conservation

Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Visakhapatnam, EventsKart is a globally recognized educational consultancy specializing in student engagement programs, international ocean conferences, and customized sustainable development initiatives for the education and eco-tourism sectors.

Driven by a mission to deliver impactful education and responsible eco-tourism solutions, EventsKart actively contributes to addressing global environmental challenges. As a Ministry of Tourism–registered consultancy, we provide specialized training in sustainable practices for the hospitality and tourism industries, while curating immersive guided tours across Southeast Asia and Europe.

Our Team

Founder

Ms. Kalyani Rao

"Bring more greens… to build more blues."

Kalyani Rao Bali is a global education entrepreneur, consultant, and sustainability project designer with over 18 years of experience spanning education, information technology, electronics, and environmental initiatives. A multilingual international speaker and mentor, she is dedicated to inspiring young minds and leading impactful Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) initiatives, including beach clean-up drives, plantation programs, and environmental awareness campaigns across the globe.

Her expertise lies in management, branding, strategic project design, and sustainable development solutions. Passionate about climate action, biodiversity conservation, marine ecosystem protection, eco-tourism, and green education, Kalyani actively advocates for innovative environmental practices and responsible tourism.

She is professionally trained as a Blue Standard Consultant and a Climate Reality Leader from the USA, and is also certified in Solid Waste Management by CIE India.

Currently, she serves as:

  • Founder of EventsKart
  • Vice President of Global Oceanic Adventures & Environmental Conservation Foundation
  • Indian Country Ambassador & President of International English Language Teachers Association
