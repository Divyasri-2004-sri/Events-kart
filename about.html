Operating at the Intersection

EventsKart operates at the intersection of environmental conservation, professional training, and global tourism — hosting educational campaigns and workshops designed to bridge knowledge gaps and inspire action towards a sustainable future.

Who We Are

Awareness is the foundation of conservation

EventsKart is an established global educational consultancy firm founded in 2021, with a primary base in Visakhapatnam, India. We specialize in student engagement programs, global ocean conferences, and customized sustainable development projects for the education and eco-tourism sectors.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education and eco-tourism services that actively address the environmental crisis. As a Ministry of Tourism registered consultant, EventsKart facilitates specialized Sustainable Practices trainings for the hospitality industry and tourism departments, and designs guided tours across South East Asia & Europe.
